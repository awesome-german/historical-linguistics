## Introduction

Historical linguistics of German traces the fascinating evolution of the German language through various stages, from its Germanic roots to its modern standard form. This list compiles essential resources for students, researchers, and enthusiasts interested in understanding how German has changed over more than a millennium.

## Historical Periods

### Old High German (750-1050)

Resources focusing on the earliest documented stage of the German language.

- [Dictionary of Old High German](https://www.saw-leipzig.de/de/projekte/althochdeutsches-woerterbuch) - Comprehensive dictionary project documenting OHG vocabulary.
- [Althochdeutsch in St. Gallen](https://www.stgallplan.org/de/index_plan.html) - Digital resources from St. Gallen monastery manuscripts.
- [The Online Textbook of Old High German](http://www.medieval-studies.net/OHG/) - Free online learning resource for OHG.

### Middle High German (1050-1350)

The classical period of medieval German literature.

- [Mittelhochdeutsche Begriffsdatenbank](http://mhdbdb.sbg.ac.at/) - Database of Middle High German concepts and terms.
- [Mittelhochdeutsche Wörterbücher im Verbund](https://www.woerterbuchnetz.de/MWV/) - Network of MHG dictionaries.
- [Nibelungenlied Manuscript](http://www.nibelungenlied-gesellschaft.de/) - Resources on the famous epic poem.

### Early New High German (1350-1650)

Period of standardization and the Lutheran Bible translation.

- [Frühneuhochdeutsches Wörterbuch](https://fwb-online.de/) - Early New High German dictionary online.
- [Luther Bible Digital](https://www.die-bibel.de/bibeln/unsere-uebersetzungen/lutherbibel/) - Resources on Martin Luther's translation.
- [Printing Revolution Resources](https://www.gutenberg-museum.de/) - Impact of printing on language standardization.

### New High German (1650-present)

Modern standard German and its contemporary variations.

- [DWDS - Digital Dictionary of German](https://www.dwds.de/) - Comprehensive modern German dictionary with historical references.
- [Atlas zur deutschen Alltagssprache](http://www.atlas-alltagssprache.de/) - Atlas of everyday German language variations.
- [IDS Mannheim](https://www.ids-mannheim.de/) - Institute for the German Language research portal.

## Core Resources

### Books

- **Geschichte der deutschen Sprache** by Wilhelm Schmidt - Comprehensive overview of German language history.
- **Deutsche Sprachgeschichte** by Peter von Polenz (5 volumes) - Authoritative work on German language history.
- **A History of the German Language** by John Waterman - English-language introduction to German historical linguistics.
- **Althochdeutsch** by Wilhelm Braune / Ernst Ebbinghaus - Standard reference grammar for OHG.
- **Mittelhochdeutsche Grammatik** by Hermann Paul - Classic MHG grammar.

### Academic Papers

- [Zeitschrift für deutsches Altertum und deutsche Literatur](https://www.hirzel.de/zeitschrift/zeitschrift-fuer-deutsches-altertum-und-deutsche-literatur/) - Leading journal for older German language and literature.
- [Beiträge zur Geschichte der deutschen Sprache und Literatur (PBB)](https://www.degruyter.com/journal/key/BGSL/) - Premier journal for historical German linguistics.
- [Journal of Germanic Linguistics](https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/journal-of-germanic-linguistics) - International journal covering Germanic linguistics.

### Online Courses

- [Old High German Course - University of Vienna](https://www.univie.ac.at/) - Free online materials for learning OHG.
- [Middle High German Summer School](https://www.mediaevum.de/) - Intensive courses in medieval German.
- [Coursera - Germanic Languages](https://www.coursera.org/) - Various courses on Germanic language history.

## Digital Resources

### Dictionaries and Tools

- [German-English Dictionary App](https://german-english-dictionary.devtheworld.jp/) - For scientifically-efficient modern learning.
- [Wörterbuchnetz](http://www.woerterbuchnetz.de/) - Network connecting numerous historical German dictionaries.
- [Lexer's Middle High German Dictionary](http://www.woerterbuchnetz.de/Lexer/) - Classic MHG dictionary online.
- [Deutsches Wörterbuch von Jacob und Wilhelm Grimm](http://woerterbuchnetz.de/DWB/) - The Grimm Brothers' monumental dictionary.

### Corpora and Databases

- [Referenzkorpus Mittelhochdeutsch](https://www.linguistics.ruhr-uni-bochum.de/rem/) - Reference corpus of Middle High German.
- [Referenzkorpus Althochdeutsch](https://www.deutschdiachrondigital.de/) - Reference corpus of Old High German.
- [DWDS-Kernkorpus](https://www.dwds.de/d/korpora/kern) - Core corpus of 20th century German.
- [Deutsches Textarchiv](https://www.deutschestextarchiv.de/) - German Text Archive (1600-1900).

### Websites and Portals

- [Medieval Germanic Studies](http://www.medieval-studies.net/) - Portal for medieval Germanic language resources.
- [Germania Latina](https://www.slm.uni-hamburg.de/germanistik.html) - Resources on Latin-German language contact.
- [Althochdeutsch Portal](https://glossaria.eu/) - Database of Old High German glosses.

## Linguistic Features

### Phonology

- [Second Germanic Sound Shift Resources](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=second+germanic+sound+shift) - Videos explaining consonant shift.
- **Umlaut Development** - Studies on vowel mutation across German history.
- **Diphthongization** - Resources on MHG to NHG vowel changes.

### Morphology

- **Strong vs Weak Verbs** - Historical development of verb classes.
- **Case System Evolution** - Changes in nominal declension.
- **Gender Assignment** - Historical shifts in grammatical gender.

### Syntax

- **Word Order Development** - Evolution from SOV to V2 patterns.
- **Subordinate Clause Formation** - Historical development of hypotaxis.
- **Article Development** - Grammaticalization of definite articles.

### Lexicology

- [Lehnwortportal Deutsch](https://lwp.ids-mannheim.de/) - Portal for loanwords in German.
- **French Influence** - Resources on French borrowings (17th-18th centuries).
- **Latin and Greek Influence** - Classical borrowings in German.
- **English Influence** - Modern English loanwords (Anglicisms).
